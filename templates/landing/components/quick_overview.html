Scientific challenges

Computational pathology – at the frontiers of a deeper healthcare understanding:

  • This is the digital time for histopathology, enabling the use of the enormous potential embedded within these massive high-content images, at the junction of clinics, medical imaging and omics, to deeply understand the modern challenges in personalized healthcare, as to discover new biomedical insights and treatments.

Towards a responsible AI – prevail the future of empowering inteligence:

  • In addition to their efficiency and effectiveness, modern Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques need to keep the link with the experts and the users, by providing usable methods and algorithms for an effective traceability, interpretability and explicability of their actions / suggestions, in order to ensure – ultimately – a Responsible AI.
